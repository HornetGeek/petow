# 🚀 Quick Start: Using Your Public Storefront

## For Clinic Dashboard Users

### 1. Adding Products with Images

**Step-by-step:**

1. Navigate to **المنتجات والمخزون** (Products & Inventory)
2. Click **إضافة منتج** (Add Product) button
3. Fill in product details:
   - Name (required)
   - Description
   - Category
   - Price (required)
   - Stock quantity (required)
   - SKU code (optional)
4. In **صور المنتج** (Product Images) section:
   - Click the upload area
   - Select one or multiple images
   - First image becomes the primary image
   - Hover and click X to remove unwanted images
5. Click **حفظ المنتج** (Save Product)

**Tips:**
- Use high-quality, well-lit images
- Recommended size: 800x800px or higher
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP
- First image appears on product cards and storefront

---

### 2. Sharing Your Storefront

**Your Storefront Link Section** appears at the top of the products page with:

#### Option A: Copy Link
1. Click **نسخ الرابط** (Copy Link) button
2. You'll see "تم النسخ!" (Copied!) confirmation
3. Paste the link anywhere:
   - WhatsApp messages
   - Facebook posts
   - Instagram bio
   - Email signatures
   - SMS to customers
   - Google Business Profile

#### Option B: Preview First
1. Click **معاينة المتجر** (Preview Store) button
2. Opens storefront in new tab
3. Check how customers will see your products
4. Then copy the link from browser

**Example Link:**
```
https://yoursite.com/storefront/123
```

## For Your Customers

### How Customers Use Your Storefront

#### Viewing Products
1. Click your shared link
2. Browse products in beautiful cards
3. Filter by category (Food, Accessories, etc.)
4. Search for specific items
5. View product images, prices, and stock status

#### Viewing Services
1. Click **الخدمات** (Services) tab
2. See all available services
3. View prices and descriptions
4. Click **احجز الآن** (Book Now)

#### Adding to Cart
1. Click **إضافة** (Add) on any product
2. Cart counter updates in header
3. Click cart icon to view items
4. Contact clinic to complete order

#### Contact You
- See clinic address, phone, and hours in banner
- Click phone number to call
- Click email to send message

## Troubleshooting

### "Link not working"
- Check if you copied the full URL
- Try opening in different browser
- Ensure internet connection

### "Images not showing"
- Use supported formats (JPG, PNG)
- Check image file size (max 5MB recommended)
- Re-upload if needed

### "Customers can't find products"
- Ensure products are marked as "active"
- Check product categories
- Verify search keywords in product names
